## Introduction

We explore the `gapminder` dataset that is available from the
`gapminder` R package. The dataset has 1704 observations and has 6
variables including

  - country
  - continent
  - year
  - life expectancy at birth
  - total population
  - per-capita GDP (in units of international dollars)
